About Us

Smart Designers

We are Smart Designers, your one-stop shop for transforming digital dreams into tangible creations. Whether you have a groundbreaking 3D design, a cherished photo waiting to be immortalized as a stunning lithophane lamp, or an idea itching to be etched with precision through laser engraving, we have the expertise and passion to make it happen.

Our History

Our history is rooted in the halls of Jaffna University's Faculty of Mechanical and Process Engineering. Fueled by a student's fascination with engineering, this company began with the ambition to turn knowledge into something tangible. The founder's academic grounding provided the foundation to not only build this business, but also to engineer innovative machines like laser engravers, 3D printers, and more. This journey, born from a passion for creation, is far from over. We invite you to explore our story and discover how our ever-evolving creations can help bring your ideas to life.
